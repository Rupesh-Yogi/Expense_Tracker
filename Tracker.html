About Incomex

Incomex is a web app for tracking your daily income and expenses with ease. It supports multiple
wallets and provides basic statistics to help you manage your finances.

Built with modern web technologies, Incomex is completely free to use and open for contributions from developers around the world.

Laravel Filament Open Source

All Features

Single-User Support

Designed for individual users with personalized experience and data privacy.

Custom Wallets

Create and manage multiple wallets. Manage multiple wallets separately for easier.

Basic financial statistics

Visualize your income and expenses with interactive charts and graphs to better understand your spending patterns.

Timezone and currency configuration

Adjust settings to your local timezone and preferred currency for accurate tracking.

Custom categories

Organize transactions with customizable categories for better financial organization.

Data Exportt

Export your financial data in CSV formats for easy backup and analysis.

Manual Database Backup

Back up your database manually by downloading a complete .sql file.
